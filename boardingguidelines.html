Check-In and Boarding Guidelines

  • To avoid long lines at the airport, check in online ahead of time. More information about quick check-in choices may be found here.
  • All check-in counters close 60 minutes prior to the planned departure time.
  • Only the group leader should come forward to check-in if traveling in a group. In the same way, only one guest per booking is required to check in.


Red Eye Flights

Please be reminded that if your flight departs after midnight (e.g., 0040H, 0125H, etc. ), you must check in the day before the date specified on your Itinerary Receipt.



Take Note!

  • Check-in your bags at the Bag Drop Counter before the counter closes. You must have your travel documentation confirmed if you are flying internationally.
  • Arrive at your assigned boarding gate at least 45 minutes before to the scheduled departure time.
