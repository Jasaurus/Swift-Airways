Check-in Options: Web/Mobile




Online Check-in
  • For international flights, tickets are available from seven days up to four hours before departure.
  • For domestic flights - available from 7 days up to 1 hour before your flight


Online Check-in is not allowed for:
  • Minors who are unaccompanied (guests below 12 years old who are traveling alone).
  • Guests who are traveling with a child.
  • Guests with disabilities or who require special help.



To obtain a boarding pass, please head to our check-in desk.

Take Note!

  • Swift Airway web and mobile boarding cards are accepted in all Philippine airports for domestic flights departing from the following airports: Batanes, Coron, Maynila, Ormoc, and Lingayen.
  • Arrive at your assigned boarding gate at least 45 minutes before to the scheduled departure time.
  • Seats are assigned automatically, however for a price, you can alter and choose your seat.
  • Have you made any last-minute changes? Even after you've checked in, you can still manage your reservation.
